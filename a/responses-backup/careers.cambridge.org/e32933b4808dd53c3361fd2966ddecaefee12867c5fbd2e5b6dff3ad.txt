<div class="earcu_posdescriptionContainer"><div class="earcu_posdescription"><div class="earcu_posdescriptionnote">
<p>Cambridge is looking for a full-time Junior Software Engineer to be part of our Academic Technology Team, responsible for developing and maintaining software applications by studying business operations and working closely with peers for the actual technical requirements of the application.</p>
<p><strong>Why should you join Cambridge?</strong></p>
<p>Cambridge University Press and Assessment is a global company working under the umbrella of the University of Cambridge in the United Kingdom. The Philippine hub is located in Makati City, running to support the University's global publishing and assessment operations.</p>
<p>When you join Cambridge, you get the best possible start in the best possible career in IT, and with benefits to match. As a global institution, our mission is to unlock our people's potential by providing all the knowledge and tools essential for your role and creating an environment that will enable you to perform at your peak.</p>
<p><strong>What can we offer you?</strong></p>
<p>We have 50 offices worldwide, with ten major ‘hubs.' In this role, you will work and collaborate with colleagues from our UK headquarters and hubs in New York, Singapore, Australia, India, etc. &nbsp;It is an opportunity that will enable you to widen your horizon by learning from other cultures.</p>
<p>If you are a new graduate of an IT, software related degree, we strongly encourage you to apply for this position. This is a hands-on technical development role, and you can become a highly proficient software engineer in at least one web/application development area. You will also take responsibility for small to medium-sized projects, and you will be working together with mid/senior-level software engineers. In addition, you will also be given training and certification that are essential for your role.</p>
<p>On top of these opportunities, working with Cambridge will also give you stability. We show our care for our people by allowing them to grow not just professionally but also personally. We promote work-life balance through flexible work schedules, hybrid work arrangements, and generous paid leaves. In addition, you will be entitled to our health care benefits with coverage for dependents,&nbsp; group life insurance, and robust wellness programs right on the first day of joining the company.</p>
<p><strong>What will you do as a Junior Software Engineer?</strong></p>
<p>You will be working in a development environment with continuous integration process, unit testing and peer reviews. Your primary tasks include writing defect-free and well-designed codes, testing and troubleshooting programs while utilizing the appropriate hardware, database, and programming technology.</p>
<p>Your role involves working with a range of stakeholders: the Scrummaster, product owner and team lead, and teams such as&nbsp; QA group, Content Services Group, SRE group, SA Group and the Academic Business both within the UK and Manila.</p>
<p>With you in the team, Cambridge will be able to provide you with the experience to work on&nbsp;software development and support&nbsp;for our online digital products using industry-leading technologies.</p>
<p><strong>What are the qualifications?</strong></p>
<p>We are looking for candidates with 1-2 years experience in software development competencies within an area of web technologies such as Java, J2EE, JSF CSS, and Spring-Struts framework.</p>
<p>In addition, experience, knowledge or expertise in the following are an advantage:</p>
<ul>
<li>HTML, CSS, Javascript, Node.js, PHP, Wordpress</li>
<li>Database technology such as Mysql, Oracle</li>
<li>GIT source code control systems</li>
<li>AWS</li>
</ul>
<p>An ideal candidate for this role also possesses the essential skills to become a future team leader and decision-maker including organizational skills, communication skills, and the ability to work with tight deadlines while continuing to deliver quality product.</p>
