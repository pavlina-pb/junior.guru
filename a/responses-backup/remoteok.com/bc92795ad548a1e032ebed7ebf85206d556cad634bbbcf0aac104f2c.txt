<div class="markdown">\nDevelopment @ FieldRoutes\n\nOn the FieldRoutes software development team, code is released daily to tens of thousands of field service staff around the country (our customers' service about 4% of homes in America)&mdash;the code you write in the morning could be deployed to thousands of users the same day. Our office software runs on a LAMP stack deployed across self-hosted cloud servers running the same codebase, and our app uses React Native with a continuous integration solution for a clean, build-less release process.\n\nThere are lots of differently shaped problems to take on at FieldRoutes, and we are looking for engineers who like challenges, are good problem solvers, and can build on an existing framework to optimize for scale. Customer growth presents interesting challenges&mdash;you can find something that interests you, with the opportunity to pioneer and take ownership of key features while you grow with the company. Whether you're interested in front-end user experience, mobile app development, query optimization, dev-ops, application security, or something else entirely, you'll be able to find your place.\n\nThe Software Engineer role is a direct-hire, remote position ideal for strong communicators with the ability to be detail oriented and work under pressure in a fast-paced environment. Candidates should also have the ability to track down, solve, and release solutions to bugs and issues.\n\nDuties and Responsibilities\n\n\n* \n\nImplement complex and core functionality within our platform related to scheduling, payments, billing, route optimization, automated tasks, internal/external APIs, etc.\n\n\n* \n\nFollow existing architecture and provide suggestions for improvements\n\n\n* \n\nPropose and implement new systems and processes\n\n\n* \n\nWrite scripts to alter business-critical datasets with millions of rows of data\n\n\n* \n\nPerform code reviews for other developers to ensure best practices are followed\n\n\n* \n\nHelp mentor, coach, and develop junior members of our development team\n\n\n* \n\nAssist with the recruitment of development new hires\n\n\n* \n\nWork with the latest technologies and tools to solve the problems that affect our customers\n\n\n* \n\nOptimize and tune existing features with poor performance\n\n\n\n\n\nRequired Qualifications\n\n\n* \n\nPrior working experience with working in a software company or working with a client-facing web application\n\n\n* \n\nWorking knowledge of the implementation of complex and core features in a mature codebase\n\n\n* \n\nStrong knowledge of business-critical systems (such as banking, CRM, or field services)\n\n\n* \n\nStrong knowledge of databases and ability to write complex and mission-critical SQL/MySQL queries\n\n\n* \n\nStrong knowledge of git\n\n\n* \n\nKnowledge of front-end technology\n\n\n* \n\nStrong understanding of an Agile work environment and Agile SDLC\n\n\n* \n\nStrong understanding of version control and deployment strategies\n\n\n\n\n\nPreferred Qualifications\n\n\n* \n\nBachelor's degree in Computer Science or related field\n\n\n* \n\nProficiency in one or more RDBMS databases: Oracle, MS SQL Server, Postgres. PHP preferred\n\n\n* \n\nAdvanced proficiency with OOP languages such as Python, Java, and C#. PHP preferred\n\n\n\n\n\nSome of Our Perks\n\n\n* Competitive compensation with starting salary ranging between $65,000.00 - $70,000.00 depending on experience \n\n* \n\nGenerous Paid Time Off &ndash; 18 days to start and 12 annual paid holidays\n\n\n* \n\nBest-in-class health, dental and vision coverage, including Employee-only coverage options fully paid by the company and HSA options with company contributions\n\n\n* \n\n100% Company Paid Benefits including Life Insurance, Short Term Disability, and Long-Term Disability programs\n\n\n* \n\nTraditional and Roth 401(k) Programs\n\n\n* \n\nProgressive paid family (maternity/paternity) and adoption leave\n\n\n* \n\nInternal growth and development opportunities\n\n\n\n \n\n#Salary and compensation\n
